# 🚀 Project Overview

## CHAPTER I  
## INTRODUCTION

The rental property management system plays a vital role in handling the day-to-day operations of apartments, boarding houses, and other rental units. Traditionally, property managers are responsible for collecting rent, keeping track of tenants, addressing maintenance requests, and ensuring accurate financial records. However, most of these processes are still done manually, relying on memory, notebooks, or simple digital tools like Excel to track records.

This challenge is particularly evident in **ABC Property Management Company in Metro Manila**, where the growing number of tenants and rental units makes it difficult to maintain accurate records and efficiently track maintenance. As operations scale, the need for a structured and automated system becomes essential. A well-designed platform can streamline processes, reduce errors, and improve the overall experience of property owners, managers, and tenants.

Despite its importance, property managers continue to struggle with record-keeping and organization. Rental payment histories are often remembered mentally or inconsistently recorded, leading to confusion or disputes. Maintenance requests are poorly tracked, with no clear documentation of costs or frequency. This lack of systematic documentation reduces accountability and transparency.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution: ForeRent

To address these challenges, **ForeRent** is introduced as a web-based platform designed for property owners, managers, and tenants.

### Key Capabilities:
- **Property Owners**
  - Manage properties and units  
  - Track occupancy and payments  
  - Assign managers  
  - Upload legal documents  
  - View AI-based rent predictions  

- **Property Managers**
  - Manage tenants and units  
  - Handle payments and utility bills  
  - Monitor maintenance requests  
  - Conduct inspections with e-signatures  

- **Tenants**
  - View billing and lease details  
  - Track rent and utilities  
  - Submit maintenance requests  
  - Access contracts and inspection records  

The system also includes a **messaging feature with automated replies** for common concerns, improving communication efficiency.

---

## 🤖 AI Integration

ForeRent goes beyond basic management by integrating **machine learning algorithms**:

- **Hierarchical Clustering**
  - Groups similar properties and maintenance issues  
  - Enables predictive maintenance planning  

- **Multiple Regression**
  - Predicts rental pricing  
  - Forecasts revenue and maintenance costs  
  - Uses variables such as:
    - Living area  
    - Unit capacity  
    - Amenities  
    - Historical income  

These features provide **data-driven insights** to maximize property performance.

## 📚 Project Context

Rental property management is essential in the real estate industry but is often hindered by outdated manual systems. In the Philippines, studies have shown that:

- Manual processes lead to inefficiencies and errors  
- Disorganized records cause tenant disputes  
- Poor documentation affects compliance with:
  - National Building Code (P.D. 1096)  
  - Fire Code (R.A. 9514)  

Stakeholders experience:
- **Owners/Managers** → administrative burden and inefficiency  
- **Tenants** → frustration with unclear billing and slow responses  

This highlights the need for a **centralized, intelligent system**.
